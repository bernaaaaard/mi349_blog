Hello, and welcome to my blog! This blog was made for my MI-349 class. It will contain some of my ramblings about worldbuilding ideas I have for a sci-fi setting I have been thinking about, mainly because I don't have much else to talk about. I suck at art, so I like to extapolate backstories, biologies, and cultures based off of Alien Portraits and Scenarios from the games Stellaris and The Fermi Paradox.
All art from these games on this website belong to their respective developers and publishers, I do not own these portraits.
Some things you can expect to find in this blog include:

  • Speculative Biology
  • Fictional Historical Accounts
  • Governmental Structure
  • Accounts of Fictional Wars
  • ...And Much More!

My name is Jonathan Grama and I am a game design Major at MSU. I am into many nerdy hobbies and Worldbuilding is only one of them. I really had no idea what to do for this blog, so I just went with something I had been intending to do anyways, build a sort of repository for my WorldBuilding Ideas in a permanent place.
